Our Team


The pursuit of excellence is central to K.O.T.'s mission, and we maintain high standards of achievement. To advance this mission, our highly-qualified team members provide the instruction and practice needed to make the training experience productive and effective.


* Trainers :

Dr. Ahmad E. Alhouli

Dr. Alhouli holds a PhD in Early Childhood Education from University of Pittsburgh, Pittsburgh, PA., USA and an MA in Early Childhood Education from the University of Toledo, OH. Toledo, USA. He is an associate professor of education in the Dept. of Educational Foundation, College of Basic Education, in the Public Authority for Applied Education and Training. He also received training in curriculum development, including curricula designed for students with special needs and modern teaching methods.


Dr. Eisa M. Al-Kandari

Dr. Al-Kandari received his PhD degree in Educational Management and Planning from the University of Leeds, UK. He is presently an assistant professor of educational management in the Dept. of Educational Foundation, College of Basic Education in the Public Authority for Applied Education and Training. Dr. Al-Kanadri is specialized in strategic planning at the institutional level. His area of expertise includes managerial development in general and managerial processes such as planning, leadership and supervision. Dr. Al-Kandari presents training courses and workshops on these areas as well as other areas related to human development.


Dr. Hadeel Y. Alshatti

Dr. Hadeel Alshatti obtained a PhD in Education and Communication, an MA in Comparative Education from Newcastle University, UK and an MSc in Educational Management from Portsmouth University, UK. She is currently an associate professor of education in the Dept. of Educational Foundation in the College of Basic Education, PAAET.


Dr. Hanan A. Taqi

Dr. Hanan A. Taqi holds a Ph.D. in Phonology and an MA degree in TESOL from Newcastle University - UK. She is currently an associate professor of Phonetics and Phonology in the Dept. of English, College of Basic Education in the Public Authority for Applied Education and Training. Dr. Taqi's area of expertise in the field of sounds, and interest in the psychology associated with sound production has combined to form a passion for the concept of Public Speaking. She has been trained as a professional debating judge, and participated as a judge in several debating tournaments in PAAET and Kuwait University.


Ms. Hanan M. Safar

Ms. Safar received her Bachelors in Arabic Education for Middle and High School from Kuwait University, and she has been working as a teacher of Arabic for high schooler in several public schools in Kuwait. Ms. Safar is also a trainer certified by the Arab Board, ISTD and IOA. She has implemented several workshops and training courses in her field of expertise.


Ms. Hawraa A. Al-Bloushi

Ms. Al-Bloushi obtained her Bachelors in Biology/Geology Education for Middle and High School from the College of Education in Kuwait University. She is a trainer certified by the American Board (BITA) and received special training in mental maps and warming in active learning.


Dr. Khaled M. Shuqair

Dr. Shuqair received his PhD in Renaissance Literature from the University of Toledo, OH, USA and his MA in Comparative Literature from the University of Jordan. He is currently an associate professor of Literature in the Dept. of English, College of Basic Education in the Public Authority for Applied Education and Training. He is also an accomplished translator with more than 12 books he translated in the various fields of knowledge. During his PhD years, he worked as teacher in a high school in Toledo Ohio and was the teacher and coach of high school speech and debate. Over the last three years, he has been assuming the responsibilities of coaching the Debate Teaming representing PAAET in Kuwait Debate National Tournament for Higher Educational Institutions. The team has achieved glorious results. In addition to his coaching responsibilities, he is also one of the founders of the Debate Center of PAAET, established with the vision of teaching students speaking, debate and critical thinking skills.


Ms. Mariam A. Al-Abdullah

Ms. Mariam Al-Abdullah received her Bachelors in Geology and she also hold an MA in Educational Supervision. She is a founder and a member of Akoon for Consultation and Training and a member in the Center for Training Team of Hawally Educational Area. Ms. Mariam Al-Abdullah is a trainer certified by the International Training and Development Academy (ITDA) and the Canadian Scientific Center and an expert in family coaching.


Ms. Muna F. al Mutairy

Ms. Al Mutairy received her Bachelors in Psychology from Kuwait University and her Masters in Psychological Counselling from Hull University in UK. She also holds a Diploma in Clinical Psychology-Psychological Therapy for Children and Adolescents from North Eastern University, USA. She is a specialist in family therapy and behavioral and cognitive therapy. She has been a psychological therapist for children and adolescents in the Social Development Office of the Council of Ministers General Secretariat since 1997 and a member in the Committee of Consultants for Kuwait Center for Autism. Ms. Al Mutairy gives specialized training courses and workshops in play therapy and posttraumatic therapy as well as other areas related to psychological therapy and counseling.


Ms. Nouf Al Behairi

Ms. Nouf Al Behairi holds an MA degree in Applied Linguistics/TESOL from Newcastle University. She is currently a language instructor in the College of Education, Kuwait University. Ms. Al Behairi presented several workshops and training courses on technology and learning and management of learning systems in Kuwait and other countries, like Turkey, Italy, UK and Spain. Ms. Shaikhah M. Aljuwaisri

She has been presenting training courses and workshops since 2014.


Dr. Talal Al-Mesad

Dr. Al-Mesad obtained his PhD and MA degrees in Early Childhood Education and Counselling from University of Toledo, OH. USA. He is currently an associate professor of Early Childhood Education in the Dept. of Educational Foundation, College of Basic Education, the Public Authority for Applied Education and Training (PAAET). In addition to his teaching responsibilities, he is also director of the Alumni and Job Market Office in PAAET. He also works as a counsellor and psychological therapist in Social Development Office of the Council of Minsters General Secretariat and director of Al-That Center for Psychological and Social Consultations. Dr. Al-Mesad presents training courses and workshops and hosts TV shows in the following areas: methods of modern parenting, behavioral disorders in early childhood, psychological counselling for children, kindergarten teacher-preparation and educational programs for early childhood.


Mr. Yousef A. Al-Kandrari

Mr. Yousef Al-Kandari is a teacher of psychology and a researcher in the field of education. He is a certified trainer and has presented training courses and workshops in the following areas: time management, the impact of parents on marital compatibility and raising adolescents.
